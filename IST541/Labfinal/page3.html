How to
To start Meditation first:
  1. Finding a quite spot
  2. Sitting in a comfortable place
  3. focus on breathing through your stomach (inhale and exhale)
  4. Notice your thoughts, and just observe them. Instead just focus on breathing
  5. Trying this for 5 minutes a day

note This is a process and will take time for you to see the effect.
